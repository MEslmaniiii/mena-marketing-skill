## What Is This?

**mena-marketing-skill** is a hands-on marketing curriculum built specifically for Arabic-speaking professionals, founders, and marketers operating in the MENA region.

Every track is built around **tasks, not theory**. You open a file, read the brief, and produce real output — a post, a campaign, a report, an ad — not a summary of what you read.

---

## Who Is This For?

- Founders building their first GTM in Saudi Arabia, UAE, or Egypt
- Marketers transitioning from traditional to digital channels
- Growth teams launching Arabic-first products
- Junior marketers who want portfolio-ready work fast
- Anyone who's tired of courses that teach definitions instead of skills

## How to Use This

### Option A — Follow a Track (Recommended)
1. Pick a track based on your goal
2. Open the track's `README.md` to understand the learning path
3. Work through tasks in order (01 → 02 → 03...)
4. Save your output in `progress/your-name/track-name/`
5. Compare with `examples/` when you're done

### Option B — Task-First
1. Browse `tasks/` for standalone challenges
2. Pick any task that matches your current need
3. Complete it, save output, move on

### Option C — Use It With Claude
Ask Claude to:
- Review your output against the task brief
- Give feedback on your Arabic copy
- Roleplay as a MENA client reviewing your ad campaign
- Generate a variation of any example for you to analyze

## Language Policy

- **Arabic** is the primary language for copy tasks (copywriting, ads)
- **English** is used for strategy documents, briefs, and analytics
- **Bilingual** tasks are marked with `[AR+EN]`
- All feedback and examples support both languages
