# Task: E-commerce Growth Sprint — UAE
**Level:** ADVANCED | **Cross-Track:** Ads + Analytics + Growth | **Time:** 2–3 hours

---

## The Brief

**Brand:** Mouj — إكسسوارات بحرية فاخرة مصنوعة في الإمارات
UAE-born luxury beachwear and ocean accessories brand. Price range: AED 200–800. Sells direct-to-consumer via own website.

**Current Situation:**
- Website: 18,000 monthly sessions
- Revenue: AED 85,000/month
- CVR: 1.1%
- ROAS: 2.4x (target is 4x)
- AOV: AED 415
- Email list: 4,200 subscribers, but email generates <5% of revenue
- Repeat purchase rate: 11% (industry avg: 25–30%)

**Your Mission:** Grow revenue to AED 150,000/month in 60 days without increasing the ad budget.

---

## Constraints

- Paid media budget stays flat
- Cannot change the product or pricing
- You have: email platform, Meta Ads Manager, website CMS, GA4, designer (4 hours/week)

---

## Your Task — 4 Parts

### Part 1: Diagnosis (300–400 words)
What's actually broken? Prioritize 3 biggest levers. For each:
- What is the problem?
- What does it cost the business monthly (quantify it)?
- Hypothesis for why it's happening?

### Part 2: 60-Day Growth Plan
For each of your 3 levers:
- **Lever:** [name]
- **Specific Action:** (not "improve CVR" — what exactly?)
- **Timeline:** (when?)
- **Resources needed:** (design hours, copy, technical)
- **Expected impact:** (quantify)

### Part 3: Email Revenue Uplift
Email generates <5% of revenue from 4,200 subscribers. Write a 30-day plan to get email to 15–20% of revenue:
- 4 automations to set up first (with trigger and goal)
- Subject line for your first campaign (Arabic + English)
- Segmentation you'd apply to the list

### Part 4: The 60-Day Scorecard
5 metrics that will tell you at Day 60 if this sprint worked. Starting number → Target number → How to measure.
