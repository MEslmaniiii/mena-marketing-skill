# Task: SaaS Launch in Egypt — إطلاق SaaS في مصر
**Level:** EXPERT | **Cross-Track:** Growth + Copywriting + Analytics | **Time:** 3–4 hours

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Mohasib — محاسبة سحابية للشركات الصغيرة في مصر
Cloud accounting SaaS for Egyptian small businesses (5–50 employees). Features: invoicing, expense tracking, Egyptian tax compliance (VAT, payroll tax), bank reconciliation. Priced at EGP 1,200/month.

**Situation:**
- Product is ready. Beta tested with 8 customers who love it.
- Team: 2 founders + 1 part-time marketer (you)
- Budget: EGP 30,000 for first 3 months of marketing
- Target: 50 paying customers in 90 days
- Competitors: Xero (English, expensive), generic Excel sheets, local accountants

---

## The Challenge

Egypt is a complex SaaS market:
- SME owners are skeptical of software — they've been burned before
- Price sensitivity is high — EGP 1,200/month is not cheap for a small business
- Trust comes from referrals and face-to-face, not from ads
- Accountants are gatekeepers — they either recommend tools or block them

---

## Your Task — 4 Components

### Component 1: The Launch Narrative (300 words)
Write the brand story for Mohasib used on the website and in pitch meetings. Must answer:
- Why does this exist? (The founders' real reason)
- Who is the enemy? (The problem, not a competitor)
- What changes when you use Mohasib?

Write in English. It should feel like it was written by a founder who lived the problem.

---

### Component 2: 90-Day Acquisition Plan
Get 50 paying customers in 90 days with EGP 30,000. For each channel:
- Why this channel for this product/market
- Specific tactics (not "post on social media")
- Budget allocation
- Expected contribution to 50 customers

**Channels to evaluate:** Accountant partnerships, LinkedIn outreach, Facebook groups (Egyptian business communities), Google Search ads, WhatsApp-based sales, Content/SEO

---

### Component 3: Pricing & Packaging Recommendation (200 words)
Is EGP 1,200/month right for Egyptian SMEs? Recommend a pricing structure and justify it. What's the ideal trial-to-paid conversion mechanism?

---

### Component 4: The KPI Dashboard
Define 6 metrics to track weekly for 90 days. For each:
- Metric name and how to measure it
- Target at Week 4 / Week 8 / Week 12
- What triggers a strategy change if the number is off

---

## Constraints

- Acquisition plan cannot rely primarily on paid ads
- Plan must be executable by 1 person for first 30 days
- Do not recommend freemium without addressing the revenue risk

---

## After You're Done

Ask Claude: *"Play an Egyptian small business owner with 20 employees. Pitch me Mohasib. Be skeptical about the price and be honest about what would convince you."*
