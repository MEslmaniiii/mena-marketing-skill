## MENA Posting Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Instagram: 8–10pm local time (Gulf)
- TikTok: 9pm–12am
- LinkedIn: Sun–Thu, 8–10am

**Ramadan Adjustments:**
- Shift all posting 2–3 hours later
- Reduce promotional content to 15%
- Increase community/emotional content to 50%+

**Caption Length:**
- Instagram: 100–150 words Arabic
- TikTok: 50–80 words
- LinkedIn: 150–250 words
