# Task 01: Meta Ad Copy — نص الإعلان على ميتا
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 30–45 min

---

## The Brief

**Brand:** Hessa — تطبيق تعلّم اللغة الإنجليزية للكبار في السعودية
English-learning app for adult Saudi users. Lessons are 10 minutes/day, personalized to level.

**Audience:** Saudi professionals, 24–40, career-focused
**Goal:** App installs | **Offer:** 7-day free trial
**Placement:** Instagram Feed + Stories

---

## Your Task

Write **3 versions** of a Meta ad, each with a different angle:

- **Version A: Pain-led** — Open with the frustration/problem
- **Version B: Aspiration-led** — Open with the outcome/transformation
- **Version C: Social proof-led** — Open with a result or testimonial

### Each Ad Must Include:
1. **Primary Text** — 100–150 words Arabic
2. **Headline** — Bold text below creative (max 40 chars)
3. **Description** — Below headline (max 30 chars)
4. **CTA Button** — Choose from: تنزيل التطبيق / جرّب مجاناً / اعرف أكثر

---

## Constraints

- Primary text must have a strong first line — it's all they see before "مزيد"
- No generic openings: "هل تريد تعلم اللغة الإنجليزية؟" is too weak
- Each version must mention the 7-day free trial
- Dialect: Saudi/Gulf Arabic for all 3 versions
- Do NOT start with the brand name

---

## Bonus Challenge

After writing your 3 versions, write a one-paragraph **media buyer note**:
- Which version would you test first and why?
- What creative (image/video) would you pair with each?
- What audience segment would you split-test?
