# Task 02: Google Search Ad — إعلان جوجل البحثي
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 30–40 min

---

## The Brief

**Business:** Shifaa — خدمة رعاية صحية منزلية في الإمارات (تمريض، علاج طبيعي، فحوصات منزلية)
Home healthcare service in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

**Target Keyword:** `تمريض منزلي دبي`
**Goal:** Calls and form submissions from people actively searching

---

## Your Task

Write one **Responsive Search Ad (RSA)**:
- **15 headlines** (max 30 characters each)
- **4 descriptions** (max 90 characters each)

### Headline Categories to Cover:

| Category | Goal |
|---|---|
| **Keyword-match** | Include "تمريض منزلي" or "دبي" |
| **Benefit-led** | Outcome or relief ("في راحة بيتك") |
| **Trust signal** | Credential or social proof |
| **CTA** | Action-oriented ("احجز الآن — خلال ٢٤ ساعة") |
| **Offer/urgency** | Specific deal or availability |

### Description Guidelines:
Write 4 descriptions expanding on:
- What the service includes
- Why choose this over going to a clinic
- A specific reassurance (safety, credentials, response time)
- A clear CTA with a differentiator

---

## Constraints

- Every headline: max 30 characters (Arabic + spaces)
- Every description: max 90 characters
- At least 3 headlines must naturally include the keyword
- At least 2 headlines must be CTAs
- No two headlines should say the same thing

---

## Output Format

```
HEADLINES (15):
1.
2.
...
15.

DESCRIPTIONS (4):
1.
2.
3.
4.
```

---

## After You're Done

Count characters on your 3 longest headlines and 2 longest descriptions — Google will disapprove any that exceed the limit.
