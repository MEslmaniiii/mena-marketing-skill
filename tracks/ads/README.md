## MENA Paid Media Reality Check

**What works differently in MENA:**
- CPMs are higher in UAE and KSA — creative quality matters more
- Ramadan shifts everything: audience is online 11pm–3am, emotional content outperforms rational
- WhatsApp as a conversion destination often outperforms landing pages in Egypt and Levant
- Video ads perform better in Gulf markets than static
- Arabic ads get higher engagement when they use dialect, not Fusha
- Trust signals matter enormously — logos, reviews in Arabic, social proof beat claims

**Platform priorities by goal:**
- Brand awareness: TikTok (KSA/EGY), Snapchat (KSA/UAE)
- Lead gen: Meta (Instagram + Facebook) across all markets
- Intent-based purchase: Google Search (all markets)
- Retargeting: Meta + Google Display

## Ad Copy Formula for Arabic Audiences

```
Pattern Interrupt  → Something unexpected in line 1
Pain or Desire     → Name what they feel, not what you sell
Credibility Signal → Number, name, result — something verifiable
Offer              → Specific, time-bound or quantity-bound if possible
CTA                → Action verb + what happens next
```

**What kills Arabic ads:**
- Opening with the brand name
- "تسوق الآن" as the only CTA
- Stock photos of models who don't look like the audience
- Arabic that feels translated
