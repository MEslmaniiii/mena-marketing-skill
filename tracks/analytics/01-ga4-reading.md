# Task 01: Reading a GA4 Report — قراءة تقرير GA4
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** English | **Time:** 30–45 min

---

## The Brief

You're a marketing analyst for a hypothetical Saudi e-commerce brand. You've been sent the following GA4 data for February. Your manager asks: *"What happened last month and what should we focus on this month?"*

---

## Your Task

Write a **structured analysis** following this exact format:

### Section 1: What Happened (150–200 words)
The headline story. Patterns, not individual numbers.

### Section 2: Why It Happened — Hypotheses (150–200 words)
3–5 specific, testable hypotheses. State them as hypotheses, not facts.

### Section 3: Priority Actions (100–150 words)
Exactly 3 actions, in priority order. Each must be:
- Specific (not "improve mobile experience")
- Tied to a specific data point
- Actionable within 2 weeks

### Section 4: What Data Is Missing (50–100 words)
What else would you need to confirm your hypotheses?

---

## After You're Done

- Did you state a clear problem or opportunity — not just "bounce rate went up"?
- Are your recommended actions specific enough that a developer could act today?
- Ask Claude: *"Play a skeptical CMO. Challenge my analysis of this GA4 data."*
