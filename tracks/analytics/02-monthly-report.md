## Your Task

Write a complete monthly marketing report with these sections:

**1. Executive Summary** (100–150 words)
The 3 most important things that happened. What worked, what didn't, what it means.

**2. Performance by Channel** (table format)
Channel | Spend | Revenue/Result | Key Metric | vs. Last Month | Status (🟢/🟡/🔴)

**3. Wins This Month** (3–5 bullet points)
Specific wins with numbers and context.

**4. Problems & Opportunities** (2–3 items)
Problem → Cost/Impact → Recommended Fix

**5. Recommendations for Next Month** (3 priorities)
What to do, why, and expected result.

**6. Arabic Summary Block** (3–5 sentences for bilingual stakeholders)

## After You're Done
