# Task 01: Product Headlines — العناوين الإعلانية
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 30–45 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Majer — تطبيق لإدارة المصاريف الشخصية للمستخدم السعودي
A personal expense tracking app for Saudi users. Syncs with Saudi bank accounts, shows spending by category, sends monthly summaries.

**Target Audience:** سعوديون بين 25–38 سنة، موظفون، يصرفون أكثر مما يخططون ويحسون بالضغط في نهاية الشهر.

**Channel:** App Store listing + Instagram ad headline

**Goal:** Drive installs.

---

## Your Task

Write **5 headlines** for this product. Each headline must:
- Be in Arabic (Gulf/Saudi dialect acceptable for 2, Fusha for 3)
- Be under 10 words
- Say something specific — no generic quality claims
- Make the reader feel something: relief, curiosity, or recognition

---

## Constraints

- Do NOT use: "الأفضل", "مميز", "رائع", "حلول متكاملة"
- Do NOT translate from English
- One headline must use a number
- One headline must name the specific problem (overspending / آخر الشهر)
- One headline must be a question

---

## Headline Formats to Try

| Format | Example Concept |
|---|---|
| Problem statement | "خلصت الفلوس وما عرفت وين راحت؟" |
| Outcome promise | "اعرف وين يروح كل ريال" |
| Number-led | "٣ دقايق كل أسبوع تكفيك تتحكم في مصاريفك" |
| Question | "كم صرفت الأسبوع اللي فات؟" |
| Contrast | "مو بس ادخار — فهم كيف تصرف" |

---

## Output Format

```
Headline 1: [نص العنوان]
Format used: [problem/outcome/number/question/contrast]
Why it works: [1 sentence]

Headline 2: ...
```

---

## After You're Done

1. Read each headline aloud. Remove any that feel stiff or translated.
2. Pick your strongest one. Ask: would this stop a Saudi professional scrolling Instagram?
3. Check `examples/copywriting/01-product-headline-example.md` to compare.
