# Task 02: Social Media Caption — كابشن السوشيال ميديا
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 20–30 min

---

## The Brief

**Brand:** Batal — متجر إلكتروني سعودي للعناية بالرجل (عطور، سكين كير، جروميج)
An e-commerce brand for men's grooming. Saudi-based, ships GCC.

**Target Audience:** رجال سعوديون وخليجيون، 22–40 سنة، يهتمون بالمظهر، يتابعون المؤثرين في لايف ستايل.

**Channel:** Instagram caption (product photo — a new oud-based perfume)

**Goal:** Drive engagement (saves + comments) + clicks to product page

---

## Your Task

Write **one Instagram caption** for a new Oud + Amber perfume called "نخبة". Priced at 249 SAR. Limited first batch of 300 bottles.

The caption must have:
1. **Hook** — First line stops the scroll (shown before "المزيد")
2. **Story or context** — Why this product, why now, why for him
3. **Product detail** — Specific, sensory (smell, feel, occasion)
4. **Social proof or scarcity cue**
5. **CTA** — One clear action
6. **Hashtags** — 5–8 relevant, mix Arabic + English

---

## Constraints

- Total caption: 100–180 words in Arabic
- First line (hook): max 8 words — must create curiosity or emotion
- No "عروض لا تفوتك" or generic urgency phrases
- The scarcity must feel real, not manufactured panic
- At least one sensory word describing the scent or experience

---

## Output Format

```
HOOK (Line 1):
[العنوان الأول]

BODY:
[باقي الكابشن]

CTA:
[الطلب الواحد الواضح]

HASHTAGS:
[#tag1 #tag2 ...]
```

---

## After You're Done

Ask yourself:
- Would I stop scrolling for this hook?
- Does this sound like it was written by someone who uses the product?
- Is there one moment where I feel something — pride, desire, FOMO?
