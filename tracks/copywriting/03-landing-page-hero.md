# Task 03: Landing Page Hero Section — قسم الهيرو
**Level:** BUILDER | **Language:** Arabic + English [AR+EN] | **Time:** 45–60 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Sahl — SaaS منصة حجوزات للعيادات الخاصة في السوق السعودي
A booking and clinic management SaaS for private clinics in Saudi Arabia. Features: online booking, patient records, Mada/STC Pay integration, WhatsApp appointment reminders.

**Target User:** مدير عيادة خاصة أو دكتور يدير عيادته بنفسه — يعاني من الفوضى في المواعيد والأوراق.

**Page Goal:** Demo request / free trial signup

---

## Your Task

Write the full hero section in **both Arabic and English**:

1. **Pre-headline** (eyebrow text) — 4–6 words, category label
2. **Headline** — Main value proposition, 6–12 words
3. **Subheadline** — Expands on headline, speaks to the pain, 1–2 sentences
4. **CTA Button Text** — 3–6 words
5. **Trust line** — Below CTA, reduces friction

Produce both Arabic and English versions of all 5 elements.

---

## Constraints

- Headline must speak to outcome, not feature ("ترتيب مواعيدك" not "نظام إدارة العيادات")
- CTA must be active ("ابدأ مجاناً" not "للمزيد من المعلومات")
- Subheadline must name a specific daily pain point
- Trust line must remove a specific objection (cost, commitment, complexity)
- Arabic version must be written natively — not translated from English

---

```
=== ARABIC VERSION ===
Pre-headline:
Headline:
Subheadline:
CTA:
Trust Line:

=== ENGLISH VERSION ===
Pre-headline:
Headline:

CTA:
Trust Line:
```
