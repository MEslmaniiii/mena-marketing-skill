# Task 04: Email Welcome Sequence — سلسلة إيميلات الترحيب
**Level:** BUILDER | **Language:** Arabic + English [AR+EN] | **Time:** 60–90 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Hessa — تطبيق تعلّم اللغة الإنجليزية للكبار في السعودية
English learning app for adult Saudis. 10 minutes/day, personalized to level.

**Trigger:** User just signed up for the 7-day free trial.

**Goal:** Get users to complete their first lesson within 24 hours and stay active through Day 7.

---

## Your Task

Write a **3-email welcome sequence** sent over 7 days:

- **Email 1** — Sent immediately after signup. Goal: get them to open the app and complete lesson 1 TODAY.
- **Email 2** — Sent Day 3. Goal: re-engage users who haven't returned. Use a story or insight.
- **Email 3** — Sent Day 6. Goal: convert to paid subscription before trial ends.

### Each Email Must Include:
- Subject line (Arabic + English version)
- Preview text (40–60 chars)
- Body copy (150–250 words)
- CTA (single, specific)

---

## Constraints

- Email 1: warm, welcoming, low friction — do NOT mention the paid plan
- Email 2: no hard sell — use a insight, story, or user result
- Email 3: direct conversion email — mention price, what they lose if they don't upgrade, clear CTA
- All emails must be in Arabic (primary) with English subject line alternative
- No subject lines starting with the app name

---

## After You're Done

Read the sequence as a user who signed up but forgot about the app. Does Email 2 make you want to come back? Does Email 3 make you feel urgency without feeling manipulated?
