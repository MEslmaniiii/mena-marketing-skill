# Task 01: Customer Journey Map — خريطة رحلة العميل
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** English | **Time:** 45–60 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Wajd — تطبيق حجز مطاعم راقية في الرياض
An upscale restaurant reservation app for Riyadh. Target user: Saudi professional, 28–45, dines out frequently with family or for business.

---

## Your Task

Map the complete customer journey across **5 stages**. For each stage:

- **User Goal:** What does the user want at this point?
- **Touchpoints:** Where do they interact with the brand?
- **Actions:** What does the user physically do?
- **Thoughts:** What are they thinking?
- **Emotions:** How do they feel?
- **Pain Points:** What frustrates or blocks them?
- **Opportunities:** What could the brand do better here?

---

## The 5 Stages

**Stage 1: Awareness** — The user doesn't know Wajd exists yet. How do they discover it?

**Stage 2: Consideration** — They've heard of Wajd. They're evaluating whether to download or use it.

**Stage 3: First Use (Activation)** — They download and make their first reservation.

**Stage 4: Retention** — They return to use the app again (or don't).

**Stage 5: Referral / Advocacy** — They tell others about it (or don't).

---

## MENA-Specific Context to Consider

- Saudi professionals often plan dinner outings through WhatsApp group conversations
- App Store reviews in Arabic are highly trusted by this audience
- Ramadan creates a unique dining behavior shift — reservations made for Iftar, not dinner
- Instagram is where upscale restaurants build audiences — integration matters
- "Wasta" (referrals/connections) and social proof matter — seeing a friend uses the app is powerful

---

## Output

Use a table or structured sections. Make it scannable.

After the map, add a **Priority Opportunities** section — pick the 2 stages with the most leverage and write 1–2 specific recommendations for each.
