# Task 02: Acquisition Experiment — تجربة الاكتساب
**Level:** BUILDER | **Language:** English | **Time:** 45–60 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Ratib — SaaS إدارة الرواتب للشركات الصغيرة في مصر
HR and payroll SaaS for Egyptian SMBs (10–100 employees). Currently at 12 paying customers.

**Problem:** The team has been relying on founder network referrals to get customers. That's slowing down. They need a repeatable acquisition channel.

**Your Task:** Design one acquisition experiment to test a new channel.

---

## Experiment Design Framework

Write a complete experiment using this structure:

### 1. Hypothesis
"We believe that [action] will result in [outcome] for [audience] because [reason]."

### 2. Channel Being Tested
Which acquisition channel are you testing? Why this one for this product and market?

### 3. Experiment Setup
- What exactly will you do? (Be specific — not "post on LinkedIn")
- What is the control? (What are you comparing against?)
- What resources do you need? (Time, money, tools)
- Who runs it?

### 4. Timeline
- Start date
- End date
- Checkpoints (when do you check intermediate results?)

### 5. Success Metrics
- Primary metric (the one number that tells you if it worked)
- Secondary metrics
- Minimum threshold for "success"
- What does failure look like?

### 6. Budget
- Cost to run this experiment
- Maximum acceptable CPL for this to be worth scaling

### 7. If It Works / If It Doesn't
- What do you do if the experiment succeeds?
- What do you do if it fails?

---

## Constraints

- Budget for the experiment: max EGP 3,000
- Timeline: must produce results within 3 weeks
- Must be executable by 1 person
- Must be specific to the Egyptian SMB market
