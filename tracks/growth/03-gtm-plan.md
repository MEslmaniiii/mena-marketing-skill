# Task 03: GTM Plan — خطة الدخول إلى السوق
**Level:** BUILDER | **Language:** English | **Time:** 60–90 min

---

## The Brief

**Product:** Ratib — SaaS إدارة الرواتب للشركات الصغيرة في مصر
HR and payroll SaaS for Egyptian small businesses (10–100 employees). Egyptian tax compliance, Arabic UX. Priced at EGP 800/month.

**Context:** 0 paying customers. 3 months runway. Team of 4. You are the sole growth/marketing person.

**Goal:** 30 paying customers in 90 days.

---

## Your Task

Write a complete GTM plan with these sections:

### 1. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — 200–300 words
Who exactly is the first customer? Be specific:
- Company size, industry, location
- Decision-maker title and profile
- Current pain and current solution
- Trigger event (what makes them look for a solution NOW?)
- What makes them a "good" first customer

### 2. Positioning — fill this template:
> "For [target customer] who struggles with [problem], Ratib is [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [alternative], we [differentiator]."

### 3. Acquisition Channels — Priority Order
List 3–4 channels. For each:
- Why it's right for this product/market/stage
- First specific action to take this week
- Expected timeline to first result

### 4. First 30 Days — Weekly Action Plan
Week-by-week breakdown. Be specific — not "build awareness."

### 5. Days 31–90 — Milestones
What needs to be true at Day 30, 60, and 90?

### 6. Assumptions and Risks
3 key assumptions your plan depends on. If wrong, what breaks and what's the fallback?

---

## Constraints

- Max marketing budget: EGP 5,000/month
- Egypt-specific — behavior, pricing, and channels are different from Gulf
- First 10 customers must be reachable without heavy ad spend
- Plan must be executable by 1 person for the first 30 days

Ask Claude: *"Play an Egyptian SME owner with 40 employees. I'm going to pitch you Ratib. Tell me your objections."*
