# Task 01: Arabic Keyword Research — بحث الكلمات المفتاحية
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 45–60 min

---

## The Brief

**Business:** Nour — متجر إلكتروني مصري يبيع مستلزمات الأطفال (حفاضات، ملابس، ألعاب — 0 إلى 3 سنوات)

**Market:** Egypt primarily, with shipping to Jordan and KSA

**Goal:** Find 15 Arabic keywords this business should be optimizing for.

---

## Your Task

Find **15 Arabic keywords** using any keyword research tool (Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google Autocomplete).

For each keyword, document:

| Keyword (Arabic) | Monthly Volume (est.) | Difficulty (L/M/H) | Intent | Dialect or Fusha? |
|---|---|---|---|---|

**Intent types:**
- `Informational` — user wants to learn (e.g., "كيف أختار حفاضات")
- `Commercial` — user is comparing (e.g., "أفضل حفاضات للمواليد")
- `Transactional` — user is ready to buy (e.g., "شراء حفاضات اونلاين مصر")
- `Navigational` — user wants a specific brand/site

---

## Keyword Categories to Cover

Your 15 keywords must include at least:
- 3 informational (how-to, tips, guides)
- 3 commercial (best, comparison, review)
- 3 transactional (buy, price, order)
- 3 long-tail (3+ words, specific query)
- 3 question-based (كيف، ما هو، ما أفضل)

---

## Constraints

- At least 8 keywords in Fusha Arabic
- At least 3 in Egyptian dialect
- No brand names — generic category keywords only
- At least 2 must include a location modifier

---

## After Your Keyword List, Answer These 3 Questions

**Q1:** Which 3 keywords have the highest business potential and why?

**Q2:** Which keyword would you build a pillar page around first?

**Q3:** Are there keyword clusters you can see forming?

---

## After You're Done

Ask Claude: *"Review this Arabic keyword list for an Egyptian baby products e-commerce store. Which keywords are missing, and are there any that should be removed?"*
