# Task 02: SEO Content Brief — بريف المحتوى
**Level:** STARTER | **Language:** Arabic | **Time:** 30–45 min

---

## The Brief

**Business:** سهل — SaaS لإدارة المطاعم في الإمارات
A restaurant management SaaS for UAE-based restaurants.

**Target Keyword:** `أفضل برنامج إدارة مطعم`
Estimated monthly searches: 1,600 | Difficulty: Medium

**Goal:** Write a content brief for a blog article that ranks for this keyword.

---

## Your Task

Write a full SEO content brief including:

### 1. Article Metadata
- Target keyword (primary)
- Secondary keywords (3–5)
- Target URL slug
- Meta title (under 60 characters, includes keyword)
- Meta description (under 155 characters, includes CTA)

### 2. Article Structure
- Proposed H1 (must include primary keyword)
- Section outline with H2s and H3s
- Word count target
- Estimated reading time

### 3. Content Requirements
- 5 specific questions the article must answer
- What to include in the intro (first 100 words)
- What to include in the conclusion + CTA

### 4. Competitive Notes
- What the current top 3 results do well
- What angle will make this article better?

### 5. Internal Linking Suggestions
- 2–3 other pages this should link to

---

## After You're Done

- Is the H1 something a restaurant owner would actually search for?
- Is there a clear angle that differentiates from generic "top 10 software" listicles?
- Ask Claude to play the role of the writer and flag any part of the brief that's unclear.
