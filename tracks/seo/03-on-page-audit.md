# Task 03: On-Page SEO Audit — تدقيق الصفحة
**Level:** BUILDER | **Language:** English | **Time:** 45–60 min

---

## The Brief

Pick any Arabic-language page on a real MENA business website. It can be:
- A product page
- A blog article
- A category page
- A service page

The page must be in Arabic and should be targeting at least one keyword.

---

## Your Task

Conduct a complete on-page SEO audit. Check each element:

### Technical On-Page
- [ ] Title tag: includes keyword? Under 60 chars? Compelling?
- [ ] Meta description: includes keyword? Under 155 chars? Has CTA?
- [ ] URL slug: short, includes keyword, no Arabic characters in URL?
- [ ] H1: one only, includes primary keyword?
- [ ] H2/H3 structure: logical hierarchy? Secondary keywords present?
- [ ] Image alt text: descriptive, includes keyword where natural?
- [ ] Internal links: does this page link to related content?
- [ ] Page speed: does the page load fast on mobile? (use PageSpeed Insights)

### Content Quality
- [ ] Does the content answer the target query fully?
- [ ] Is the Arabic natural or does it feel translated?
- [ ] Word count: is it competitive with top-ranking pages?
- [ ] Does the intro hook the reader in the first 100 words?
- [ ] Is there a clear CTA?

---

## Output Format

For each element: Status (✅ Good / ⚠️ Needs Work / ❌ Missing) + 1-line note.

End with: **Top 3 Priority Fixes** — the three changes that would have the most SEO impact.

---
