- **Fewer competitors** in Arabic content means ranking is achievable faster
- **Search volume is real** — Arabic queries get millions of searches/month
- **Dialect complexity** — users search in dialect, but rank with Fusha
- **Voice search** is growing fast in MENA — especially conversational Arabic queries
- **Google Discover** drives significant traffic in the region

## Arabic Keyword Research Principles

**Search in dialect, optimize in Fusha.** Users often type colloquially, but rankings come from Fusha. Research both.

**Modifiers that matter in MENA:**
- Location: "في السعودية", "في دبي", "بالقاهرة"
- Pricing: "كم سعر", "بكام"
- How-to: "كيف", "طريقة", "خطوات"
- Best: "أفضل", "احسن" (Gulf)
- Review: "تجربة", "مراجعة", "رأي"

**Tools that work for Arabic:**
- Google Keyword Planner (Arabic interface)
- Ahrefs / Semrush with Arabic language filter
- Google Autocomplete — type in Arabic and observe
- AnswerThePublic in Arabic
